News

On July 1, 2016, Logic released a mixtape titled Bobby Tarantino. It is Logic's fifth mixtape, and his first since 2013's Young Sinatra: Welcome to Forever and was released unexpectedly via Logic's Twitter account. The mixtape includes singles "Flexicution" (which became his first solo Billboard Hot 100 charting single) and "Wrist" featuring Pusha T, with the production being handled mainly by Logic and 6ix.
